MetaMed

Introduced by the Armada team, the MetaMed is a one-stop online pharmacy that provides convenient and efficient services for all its customers. MetaMed caters to a variety of prescribed medications, from generic to branded, with flexible payment options, and delivery choices with just a click of your finger!

Release 1

Release 1 includes MetaMed's fundamental features such as search and login functionality.

Release 2

Release 2 covers additional MetaMed's order features such as pre-ordering and order tracker.

Release 3

Release 3 implements additional features to product recommendation, payment option, and delivery option.

OUR SERVICES

Release 1

Features

Filter Items

The MetaMed uses filter items to organize every product according to its categories, making it easier to navigate and pleasing to the eye.

Search Bar

The MetaMed has a built-in search bar so that the consumers can find whatever they wish to order from the website.

Register/Log In

Customers can make use of the register or log-in feature of the MetaMed. In this manner, their previous orders will be recorded.

Add/Remove Items

Once the customers add their desired products to the cart, they can add as many as they want and easily remove them if ever they change their minds.

Prescription Verification

Some medicine cannot be bought over the counter, like antibiotics and medications specific for an illness, customers should have prescriptions from their physicians.

Limit of Purchase

To avoid hoarding, the MetaMed offers a limit of purchase feature. This will depend on the prescription presented by the customer or what the system will allow.

Cash On Delivery

As one of the payment options, cash on delivery is the easiest one. The customer can order their desired products, wait for the package to come, and pay on the spot.

E-Wallet

For a cashless transaction, customers can opt for the E-wallet payment option. They can link their chosen e-wallet accounts (GCash, PayMaya, PayPal, etc.)

Debit/Credit Card

Another alternative for a cashless transaction is linking their debit and credit card accounts to the website. Note that the system will not use any information.

Receipt

Customers can view their total order at the end of the transaction, they can review them, add or remove products, and check them out once decided.

Local Delivery

The MetaMed offers a local delivery using any of the delivery companies available at service. Sipping fee and time interval between the pharmacy and customer vary.

International Delivery

The MetaMed also offers international delivery for all customers overseas. Again, the shipping fee and time of pharmacy also varies.

OUR SERVICES

Release 2

Features

Pre-order

Customers can pre-order their chosen products if they want to stock up on their maintenance. Pre-ordered items can cause some delays in shipping time.

Order Tracker

Once the order has been placed, customers can track their package. The MetaMed offers an order tracker so that the customers can see where is their package and how long before they can receive it.

OUR SERVICES

Release 3

Features

Product Recommendation

Loyal customers who order at MetaMed from time to time, can see some product recommendations. Items that are related to, what you may like, or similar to the ones they have ordered before.

Duplicate Previous Orders

Customers can purchase the items they bought from the MetaMed again. They can just simply add them to the cart one more time and check them out.

International Remittances

For international customers, they can opt for international remittance. Since the MetaMed does not accept other currencies besides the Peso, overseas customers can ship their money to any local remittance shop and pay their orders.

Express Delivery

If customers need any of the offered products as soon as possible, the MetaMed offers express delivery. This may cost additional payment express shipping but do not worry because the package will be sent to the customer immediately.
